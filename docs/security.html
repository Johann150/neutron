Privacy in the desktop variant

An important aspect of education software is data privacy. Neutron does not record or collect any type of data except for what has been written/drawn.

Privacy in the online variant

When you use Neutron in the browser, we will collect no data. Neutron does not even use any cookies. Everything you write/draw is stored locally on your computer. The progamm will not send any personal information about you or your computer back to the server. It will also not send anything that you have written/drawn back to the server.

Changes to the privacy policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy from time to time to ensure that it complies with current legal requirements or to implement changes to our services in the privacy policy, such as introducing new services. Your new visit will be subject to the new privacy policy.

A simple way to educate digitally.

Neutron is an application for windows based whiteboards. With this software it is possible to educate in the time of digitisation without sparing one of the most important tools: The blackboard.
